Frequently Asked Questions

Introduction​

What is Reedroux?​

Reedroux is a feature-rich, high-performance Discord bot. It is designed to eliminate any need for multiple bots and to provide the best companion experience in your server.

How can I add Reedroux to my Discord Server?​

Please click here to check out our guide on adding Reedroux.

Basic Information​

How can I change Reedroux's nickname in my server?​

note

Make sure you have the Manage Nicknames permission!

  1. Right Click Reedroux's name and press Change Nickname. A menu will pop up and ask you to enter your desired name.
  2. Type in your desired nickname.
  3. Press Save and you're good to go.

What are the different entries in the me ping command?​

EmojiMeaning
PingMessage response time
APIAPI response time
DatabaseDatabase response time

Does Reedroux save its settings when re-inviting the bot?​

Yes, all Reedroux's settings will be saved

Limitations/Restrictions​

Can I change Reedroux's Profile Picture/Avatar in my server?​

No, Discord does not allow bots to change their profile picture/avatar per server.

Can I save or create playlists with Reedroux?​

Yes! Reedroux does support the creation of custom playlists. Find out more about it here

What music sites does Reedroux currently support?​

These are the current sites that music can be played from.

  1. Spotify
  2. SoundCloud
  3. Apple Music
  4. Tidal
  5. Reddit
  6. Twitch
  7. Vimeo
  8. BandCamp
  9. Deezer
  10. Facebook
  11. Getyarn
  12. Yandex Music
  13. Odysee
  14. Clyp
  15. OcRemix
  16. Direct Links

Can I make custom commands for Reedroux?​

No, we do not allow custom commands/aliases.

Can Reedroux stream the video of the song that is being played?​

No, Discord does not allow bots to use the Go Live feature.

Can Reedroux play 24/7 Music?​

Yes! And it's 100% free to do so. Just use the me 247 command.

Can Reedroux execute commands given by other bots?​

No, Reedroux doesn't respond to commands issued by other bots as it can be easily abused. This restriction will not be removed, nor will any exceptions be made.

Can Reedroux play music in Stage Channels?​

Yes, all you need to do is making Reedroux a Stage Moderator. All commands work exactly the same when you use Reedroux in a normal voice channel.

What is Reedroux's Discord Server used for?​

Reedroux's Discord Server is a place built for everything related to Reedroux!

  1. Get help and support from our lovely Staff Members!
  2. You will be informed about Reedroux's new features and updates.
  3. Stay up-to-date with news and announcements.
  4. Make new feature suggestions for Reedroux.
  5. Hang out with our amazing growing community members and staff!
  6. Join and enjoy our events that take place!

To join our server, click here.
